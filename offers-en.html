Yoga with Christine

Programs

In order to be able to respond more individually to specific needs, I propose a set of programs based on key themes. These different programs include private classes specifically designed for you, depending on your physical (degree of flexibility, eventual joint pain) and emotional fitness, after an individual interview.

I can accompany you in Spanish, French, German or English.

Conquer my insomnia

Like many people, you have insomnia problems and you would like to get out of that hellish cycle of sleepless nights. The "Beat Insomnia" program is the solution for you.

The program includes:
  • A 30 minute private interview to get to know you and assess your needs
  • 4 private classes of 60 minutes by Zoom
  • 4 videos recorded of 20-30 minutes with exercises to do at home
The aim is that after this month practicing yoga together you will have a toolkit to recover a deep and restful sleep.

Classes include:
  • Soft poses that promote sleep
  • Breathing exercises
  • Mudras (hand gestures)
  • Self-massages
  • Meditations
The interval between two classes is at least a week to perfect the practice.
The rhythm of sleep does not change overnight. Changing bad habits takes time and discipline to do the exercises on a daily basis. A daily practice of 30 minutes is the guarantee of success.

Having previous yoga experience is an advantage, but not a necessity.

Price of the program: € 129
You can pay by Bizum, Paypal or bank transfer.
