Is Yoga for me?

Yoga is beneficial for all of us. You should neither have a perfect body, nor be young, flexible or athletic. Mastering your breathing is already doing yoga.

There are a large number of different practices, each with its own specificities, objectives and benefits. The trick is to identify the yoga that suits you best. The benefits of yoga are numerous and more and more scientific studies prove it.

What type of yoga?

I mainly teach Hatha Yoga with the fundamental basis of body alignment and the safe and benevolent execution of postures.

To enrich my classes or for specific needs I also use other types of yoga, such as Restorative Yoga (a yoga using many accessories for more comfort in the postures), Yin Yoga (prolonged duration of the postures for a deep stretch of the muscles) or Kundalini Yoga (for work on the vital energy of the body).
