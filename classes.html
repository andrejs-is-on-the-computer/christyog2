Group Classes

I offer group lessons in Spanish in the YoyYoga school in the heart of Granada.

Classes include an introduction to the theme, centration, warm-up, postures (asanas), relaxation with meditation (mantras) and breathing exercises (pranayama). At the end of each group lesson, we allow 5 minutes for an exchange on feelings during the lesson, or questions on the topic of the lesson. The objective of this exchange is to create group coherence in a discipline often practiced individually.

Private Classes

  • I don't feel comfortable in a group class
  • I have physical limitations that do not allow me to participate in a group lesson (back pain, lack of flexibility, age, overweight, shoulder or wrist pain, ...)
  • I would like personalized advice or on specific postures
  • I live in Granada but don't speak spanish
  • I want to take care of myself and make myself happy by offering myself a private lesson
If you recognize yourself in one or more of these statements, a private lesson is the solution for you.
I offer 3 formulas:

  • 60 minutes private lesson at my home (Alquería del Fargue). In good weather, we can even go out on the terrace with a view of the Sierra Nevada. The cost of a session is 40 €
  • 60-minute private lesson by Zoom, for € 35. Private lesson at your home (maximum 15 km from Granada center), for 50 €.
Specific programs

To be able to respond more individually to specific needs, I offer a set of programs developed according to key themes. These different programs include private lessons developed specifically for you according to your physical (degree of flexibility, possible pain in certain joints) and emotional aptitude after an individual interview.
I can teach in Spanish, French, German or English. You can make the payment by Bizum, Paypal or bank transfer.
