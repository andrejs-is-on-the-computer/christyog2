Yoga con Christine

About

My vision

Yoga is an art of daily living. I wish to offer the participants in my classes a harmonious life through a benevolent and safe practice of yoga adapted to the needs of each student.
Yoga allows a holistic approach to our being. The word yoga means "Union". The goal is that each class activates the 5 layers of our being. Our physical body Annamaya Kosha through postures, our energy body Pranamaya Kosha through breathing practices, our mental and emotional body Manomaya Kosha through meditations, our body of knowledge Vijnanamaya Kosha through the discovery of the philosophy of yoga and our body of bliss Anandamaya Kosha through chanting mantras or Yoga nidra.
Through my classes, I also want to share the philosophical concepts of yoga. Each course is organized around a theme. This theme can be of a physical nature (opening of the hips, relaxation of the shoulders, relaxation of the neck, etc.), philosophical (yamas and niyamas, koshas, ​​etc.) or even personal development (improving sleep, daring to shine , conquer your fears, ....). Classes include an introduction to the theme, centration, warm-up, postures (asanas), relaxation with meditation (mantras) and breathing exercises (pranayama).

Benefits

Is Yoga for me?

Yoga is beneficial for all of us. You should neither have a perfect body, nor be young, flexible or athletic. Mastering your breathing is already doing yoga.

There are a large number of different practices, each with its own specificities, objectives and benefits. The trick is to identify the yoga that suits you best. The benefits of yoga are numerous and more and more scientific studies prove it.

What type of yoga?

I mainly teach Hatha Yoga with the fundamental basis of body alignment and the safe and benevolent execution of postures.

To enrich my classes or for specific needs I also use other types of yoga, such as Restorative Yoga (a yoga using many accessories for more comfort in the postures), Yin Yoga (prolonged duration of the postures for a deep stretch of the muscles) or Kundalini Yoga (for work on the vital energy of the body).

Benefits

Yoga allows you to:

  • Strengthen your physical strength to feel stable and balanced
  • Strengthen their flexibility to develop a positive and open attitude towards events
  • Learn to observe and listen to your body by increasing body awareness (proprioception)
  • Accept your body as it is with its strengths and weaknesses
  • Establish a connection with his breathing which is a gateway to our nervous system
  • Determine your life mission and set your priorities
  • Learn to be, rather than do, by living the present moment fully

Who am I?

I was born in 1961 to German parents. I grew up in Brussels in an international environment. As a young teenager, I dreamed of becoming a sports teacher. In the end my choice turned to architecture. An exciting job that I have exercised in different structures: from my own architecture studio to a company, world leader in its specialty. They were very enriching experiences. Little by little my vision of priority management in the business world was moving away from my values. There followed an intense period of reflection on what I wanted to do with my life.
Passionate about yoga since the 2000s, I have followed intense training in order to deepen my personal practice. These trainings totally changed my vision of yoga. With the discovery of philosophy, alignment in postures, conscious practice of breathing, it was a transformation on a physical level but before on a personal level. I learned that exercising yoga on the mat is a reflection of our habits in life and that by adapting its practice on the mat, we can export these benefits into everyday life. Enriched by this experience, I felt invested in sharing what I had learned so that others could also benefit from it. Since then, I have been leading yoga workshops in the beautiful city of Granada in Spain, where I settled with my husband Philippe. Today, I am fortunate to be able to devote myself completely to my two passions: yoga and ceramics.
