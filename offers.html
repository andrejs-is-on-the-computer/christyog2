Packages

To be able to respond more individually to specific needs, I offer a set of programs developed according to key themes. These different programs include private lessons developed specifically for you according to your physical (degree of flexibility, possible pain in certain joints) and emotional aptitude after an individual interview.

Conquer my Insomnia

Like many people, you have insomnia problems and would like to get out of this hellish circle of sleepless nights. The "Beat My Insomnia" program is the solution for you.

This program includes:
  • 30-minute private interview to get to know and assess your needs
  • 4 private lessons of 60 minutes by Zoom
  • 4 videos recorded with exercises to do at home
  • Sheet that includes general tips for improving sleep
The goal is that at the end of this month spent together you have a toolbox to recover a deep and restful sleep.

The courses include
  • Gentle postures that make it easier to fall asleep
  • Breathing exercises
  • Meditation
Classes are spaced at least one week apart to perfect practice between classes. The rhythm of sleep does not change overnight. Changing bad habits requires personal investment and discipline to do the exercises on a daily basis. Having previous yoga experience is an advantage, but is not required to participate in this program successfully.

Price of the pack: 129 €

My Personalized Course

You have always dreamed of having a yoga class tailored to your needs, a class that takes into account your desires, your possible physical limitations and your aspirations for progress. I suggest you make your personalized course that you can then practice at your best convenience in the comfort of your home.

This program includes:
  • 30-minute private interview to get to know and assess your needs
  • 1 private lesson of 90 minutes (60 minutes of lessons and 30 minutes of questions / answers) by Zoom during which we will practice together the lesson developed specifically for you. This will give you the opportunity to ask any questions you may have. I will be there to guide you and refine your postures
  • 1 60-minute recorded video of the course developed specifically for you

Price of the pack: 69 €
