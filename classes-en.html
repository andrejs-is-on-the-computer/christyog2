Yoga with Christine

Classes

Yoga classes include introduction to the topic, centering, warm-up, postures (asanas), relaxation with meditation (mantras) and breathing exercises (pranayama). At the end of the group classes, we allow 5 minutes for a sharing of what was experienced during the class, or questions about the topic of the class. The aim of this exchange is to create group cohesion in a discipline that is often practiced individually.
