About Yoga con Christine

  • Yoga is beneficial for all of us. You should neither have a perfect body, nor be young, flexible or athletic.
  • I mainly teach Hatha Yoga with the fundamental basis of body alignment and the safe and benevolent execution of postures.
  • I wish to offer the participants in my classes a harmonious life through a benevolent and safe practice of yoga adapted to the needs of each student.
